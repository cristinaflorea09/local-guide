# Commission & Payout Terms (Draft)

This is a practical draft you can adapt with legal counsel.

## Commission
- The platform charges a commission on each completed booking.
- Default launch commission:
  - Guides: 15%
  - Hosts (experiences): 18%
- Commission may vary by subscription tier (Free/Pro/Elite) and will be displayed in-app.

## What commission covers
- Payment processing, platform operations, customer support, and marketplace promotion.

## Payouts
- Payouts are made to the seller (Guide/Host) to their configured payout method (e.g., Stripe Connect) after a holding period.
- The platform may delay payouts for fraud prevention, disputes, refunds, or compliance.

## Refunds & chargebacks
- If a booking is refunded or charged back, the platform may reverse the seller payout (partially or fully) including fees.

## Taxes
- Sellers are responsible for declaring and paying applicable taxes (VAT/income tax).

## Changes
- The platform may update commission rates with prior notice.
